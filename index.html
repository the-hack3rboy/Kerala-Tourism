About

Welcome to the landing page for Kerala Tourism! Kerala, also known as "God's Own Country," is a state in southern India known for its scenic beauty, diverse culture, and rich history. Located on the Malabar Coast, Kerala boasts of a long coastline that stretches over 580 km, dotted with serene beaches, tranquil backwaters, and lush green forests. Kerala is also home to a variety of wildlife, including elephants, tigers, leopards, and numerous bird species. Its cuisine is equally diverse, with a blend of spices and flavors that are unique to the region. From the famous appam and stew to seafood delicacies, Kerala's cuisine is a treat for the taste buds. In addition to its natural beauty and culinary delights, Kerala is steeped in history and culture. The state is home to ancient temples, churches, and mosques that are architectural marvels. Its art forms, including Kathakali, Mohiniyattam, and Theyyam, are world-renowned for their grace and beauty. Whether you're looking to unwind on a serene beach, explore the backwaters on a houseboat, or immerse yourself in the rich culture and history of the region, Kerala has something for everyone. Come, discover the magic of Kerala!
